
Controls currently are hardcoded to Shift + U, I, O, P, J, K, L, '.' keys.

Note, that to prevent a weapon reloading bugs, drop weapon function is not allowing to drop any ammo using weapons if they have less ammo than normally this weapon gives, when is taken.
It also can give bugs with ammo amount for vauss and napalm, since the engine isn't counting ammo for those weapons on the server side and a separate server-side counter has been added into the mod.
